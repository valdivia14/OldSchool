Privacy Policy – Old School

Last updated: August 12, 2025

Old School (package: com.pocho.oldschool) is published by PochoProducciones (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what data the app processes, for which purposes, how it’s shared, and your choices.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the mobile app Old School and its support site. We do not run our own servers to store personal data. The app runs over local network (LAN); data needed for ads and measurement is processed by third parties listed below.

2. Age & Audience

  • The app includes an age gate. If a user indicates they are 12 or under, we treat the experience as child-directed: interstitial ads are disabled and Google Mobile Ads SDK is tagged accordingly (COPPA).
  • Users 13–15: ads are requested non-personalized with an appropriate Max Ad Content Rating (PG).
  • Users 16+: ads as per consent where applicable (see UMP below).
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. For requests or deletion, contact potuproduccion@gmail.com.

3. Data We Process

Data collected by the ads SDK (Google AdMob) may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID – AAID), IP address, device/OS type, language, and approximate country.
  • Ad metrics (impressions, clicks, errors) and anti-fraud signals.

App technical data:

  • Diagnostics & performance (crashes, basic events) handled by platform providers.
  • Local network for LAN multiplayer: player nicknames and local IPs are used only during the session for discovery/joining and are not sent to our own servers.

We do not access: precise location, contacts, photos/camera, microphone, SMS, calendar, or account info.

4. Purposes

  • Advertising (interstitial ads; limited by age and/or consent).
  • Measurement & safety (ad analytics, fraud/abuse prevention).
  • LAN multiplayer operation (device discovery and connection on the same network).
  • Product improvement (stability and performance).

5. Legal Basis & Consent (EU/UK)

In the EU/UK we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) where applicable. You can change your choice in system settings (by resetting the advertising ID) or by contacting us. For under-consent users we set TagForUnderAgeOfConsent and request non-personalized ads.

6. Sharing with Third Parties

We share data with:

  • Google AdMob (ads and measurement). See policies.google.com/technologies/ads and AdMob Help.
  • Google Play Services (Android platform services). See policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not sell personal data and do not share with additional intermediaries beyond the above.

7. Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Third-party providers may retain logs for periods defined in their policies. You can reset your device’s advertising ID at any time.

8. Security

We apply reasonable technical measures to protect data. No method is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, objection, restriction, or deletion. Email potuproduccion@gmail.com and we’ll respond within reasonable time. You can also reset the advertising ID and limit ad personalization in device settings.

10. International Transfers

Providers (e.g., Google) may process data in data centers outside your country. Appropriate safeguards apply (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy to reflect legal or functional changes. We will post the current version here and indicate the “Last updated” date.
